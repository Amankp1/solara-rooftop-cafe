OUR STORY

Nestled in the heart of Vancouver, Solara Rooftop Cafe offers an unparalleled dining experience where artisanal coffee meets breathtaking city views. Our rooftop sanctuary is a celebration of Pacific Northwest flavors, crafted with passion and precision.

From sunrise lattes to sunset aperitifs, we invite you to savor every moment in our carefully curated space. Each cup is a journey, each meal a memory, all set against the stunning Vancouver skyline.

Join us where the city meets the sky, and every visit becomes a cherished escape.
